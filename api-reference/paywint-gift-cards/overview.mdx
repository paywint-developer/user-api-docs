Paywint gift cards let you deliver spend-ready balances to payees without handling sensitive card data.  
You request a gift card from Paywint, share the generated link or token with the recipient, and track the card through its lifecycle with Paywint APIs.

---

## Prerequisites

1. Create the payee profile by following <code>[Create Payee](/api-reference/payees/create)</code>.  
2. Add at least one address for that payee via <code>[Create Payee Address](/api-reference/payees/create-address)</code> so Paywint can route and track the delivery.

---

## Gift Card capabilities

You can:

- Issue a new gift card for a payee with a specified amount and currency.  
- Retrieve the full gift card payload (numbers, expiration, status flags) using the issued `request_id`.  
- List every gift card that you have issued.  
- Reload an existing Paywint gift card to add more funds when the card still needs a top-up.

---

## How the gift card flow works

<Steps>

  <Step title="1. Request a new card">
    Call <code>[Issue Gift Card For Payee](/api-reference/paywint-gift-cards/issue-card)</code> with the payee identity, amount, currency, and any metadata that ties the card back to your system.
  </Step>

  <Step title="2. Deliver card details">
    The response includes a `request_id` and the encrypted card payload. Store the `request_id` and share the payload with the payee through your secure channels.
  </Step>

  <Step title="3. Track card status">
    Use <code>[List Gift Cards](/api-reference/paywint-gift-cards/list-cards)</code> to see every card for the user, or call <code>[Get Gift Card](/api-reference/paywint-gift-cards/get-card)</code> when you need the latest metadata for a specific issuance.
  </Step>

  <Step title="4. Reload when needed">
    If the card needs more value, call <code>[Reload Paywint Gift Card](/api-reference/paywint-gift-cards/reload-card)</code> to top it up without issuing a new card.
  </Step>

</Steps>

## Best practices

- Treat the `request_id` as the canonical reference for every gift card issuance.  
- Share card details with payees securely, then delete or mask raw values on your end.  
- Call `List Gift Cards` regularly to reconcile what your system issued versus what remains outstanding.  
- Use the reload API only when your compliance rules allow topping up previously issued cards.
